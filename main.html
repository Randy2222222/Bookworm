Three Blind Mice
A blinding snowstorm was not an asuspicious beginning, but Molly and Giles Davis were determined to make a go of their new guest house. How could they know that among their first guest at snowbound Monkswell Manor was a murderer-a fiend who had already killed one "Blind Mouse" and was waiting to pounce on numbers two and three?
